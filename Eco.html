An ecosystem is a combination of living and nonliving parts, with each part playing an important role. A functioning ecosystem depends on webs of interactions among all the species of plants, animals, fungi, and microbes together with rocks, soil, water, and climate. Every organism in an ecosystem occupies a niche. Its niche is how it earns its living, its role in the system.
